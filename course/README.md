# 28-Day AI Skills Certificate — Zidni Academy

**For the people of Nouadhibou. For Mauritania.**

---

## What this course is

A practical, hands-on certificate program that teaches everyday people how to use AI in their daily life and work. No technical background required. No English fluency required. Just curiosity and a phone or computer.

## Who this is for

- Students who want to learn faster and write better
- Shop owners who want to sell more online
- Traders who want better prices and faster communication
- Job seekers who want stronger CVs and interview answers
- Teachers who want to prepare lessons in minutes
- Drivers and delivery workers who want to optimize routes and income
- Entrepreneurs who want to start businesses with less money

## How it works

- **28 days**, **4 weeks**, **1 certificate**
- Each day: one short lesson + one practical exercise
- Each week: one project you can show to others
- Final day: a final project that becomes your certificate evidence

## The four weeks

| Week | Theme | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Week 1 | AI Foundations | Remove fear, build the mental model |
| Week 2 | AI for Business and Work | Turn AI into income or savings |
| Week 3 | AI Tools | Stop being a one-tool user |
| Week 4 | AI Income and Practical Projects | Ship something real |

## Certificate requirements

To earn the Zidni Academy AI Skills Certificate, you must:
1. Complete all 28 daily exercises
2. Submit all 4 weekly projects
3. Complete the final project
4. Share one piece of your work publicly (Facebook post, WhatsApp status, or with a friend)

## Language

Lessons are written in **plain Arabic** with French and English terms explained. Examples come from Nouadhibou life: markets, fishing, transport, shops, schools.
