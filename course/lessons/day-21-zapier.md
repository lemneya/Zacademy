# Day 21 — Zapier and Automation

**Goal:** Understand how to connect apps and save time with automation.

---

## What is automation?

Automation means making apps work together without you doing the work manually.

Example:
- When someone fills your Google Form, automatically send them a WhatsApp message
- When you post on Facebook, automatically save the post to your Notion page
- When you receive an email, automatically create a to-do item

## What is Zapier?

Zapier is a tool that connects different apps. It has a free plan for basic use.

## Simple automations for Nouadhibou businesses

**Automation 1: New customer welcome**
Trigger: Someone fills your Google Form (registration)
Action: Send them a WhatsApp message saying "Thank you for registering! We will contact you soon."

**Automation 2: Save Facebook leads**
Trigger: Someone comments on your Facebook post
Action: Add their name to a Google Sheet

**Automation 3: Daily reminder**
Trigger: Every morning at 8 AM
Action: Send you a WhatsApp message with today's to-do list

## How to start with Zapier

1. Go to zapier.com
2. Create a free account
3. Click "Create Zap"
4. Choose a trigger app (e.g., Google Forms)
5. Choose an action app (e.g., WhatsApp)
6. Test it

## Free alternatives

- **Make.com** — similar to Zapier, free plan available
- **Google Apps Script** — free, requires some learning
- **IFTTT** — very simple automations, free
