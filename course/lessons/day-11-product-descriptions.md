# Day 11 — Product Descriptions

**Goal:** Write product descriptions that make people want to buy.

---

## Why product descriptions matter

A good description does not just list what the product is. It paints a picture of how the customer's life will be better after buying it.

**Weak description:**
> "Red dress. Size M. 3000 ouguiya."

**Strong description:**
> "A vibrant red dress perfect for Eid celebrations or family gatherings. Lightweight fabric keeps you cool in Nouadhibou's heat. Size M. Only 3000 ouguiya — limited stock. Message now to reserve yours."

## The product description formula

Every strong description has:
1. **What it is** — clear and simple
2. **Who it is for** — the customer can see themselves
3. **Why it matters** — the benefit, not just the feature
4. **Urgency or scarcity** — why buy now, not later
5. **Call to action** — what to do next

## AI prompt for product descriptions

> "Write a product description for [product] that I sell in Nouadhibou. My customers are [describe customers]. The price is [price]. Write it in [Arabic/French], friendly and local. Include: what it is, who it is for, why they need it, and a call to action. Keep it under 100 words."

## Examples by business type

**Fish seller:**
> "Fresh red mullet caught this morning at the Port de Peche. Perfect for family lunch or restaurant supply. Cleaned and ready to cook. 600 ouguiya per kilo. Order before noon for same-day delivery in Nouadhibou."

**Phone accessories:**
> "Protect your phone screen with this tempered glass protector. Fits iPhone and Samsung models. One scratch on your screen costs 10,000 ouguiya to fix. This protector is 1500 ouguiya. Available at my shop near the Cinquième market."
