# Day 2 — ChatGPT Basics

**Goal:** Learn to open ChatGPT and have your first real conversation.

---

## What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI assistant made by a company called OpenAI. You type messages to it, and it types messages back. It can help with writing, thinking, planning, learning, and problem-solving.

## How to access ChatGPT

**Option 1 — Free version (good for starting):**
1. Go to chatgpt.com on your phone or computer
2. Create an account with your email or Google account
3. Start chatting

**Option 2 — WhatsApp (if you prefer):**
- OpenAI has a WhatsApp number for ChatGPT in some regions
- Save the number and send a message like you would to a friend

**Option 3 — Mobile app:**
- Download "ChatGPT" from the App Store or Google Play Store

## Your first conversation

Try this exact message:

> "I live in Nouadhibou, Mauritania. I run a small shop. Explain to me like I am smart but new to technology: how can AI help my business?"

Notice:
- ChatGPT answers in the same language you write in
- It remembers what you said earlier in the same conversation
- You can ask follow-up questions

## Important rules

1. **One conversation = one topic.** If you switch to a new topic, start a new chat.
2. **Be specific.** "Help my shop" is weak. "Write a Facebook post selling fresh fish from the Port de Peche" is strong.
3. **Correct it.** If ChatGPT says something wrong, tell it. It will adjust.

## Today's exercise

**Have a 15-minute conversation with ChatGPT about your work or daily life. Ask at least 3 follow-up questions. Save a screenshot of the best answer.**

Example topics:
- "How can I save time on my daily tasks?"
- "Help me write a message to a supplier"
- "Explain something I am trying to learn"
