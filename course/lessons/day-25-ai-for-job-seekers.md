# Day 25 — AI for Job Seekers

**Goal:** Use AI to find jobs, write strong applications, and interview with confidence.

---

## The job market in Nouadhibou

Jobs are competitive. A strong application makes the difference between getting ignored and getting called.

AI can help at every step:
- Finding job listings
- Writing CVs
- Writing cover letters
- Preparing for interviews
- Following up professionally

## Finding jobs with AI

**Search prompts:**
> "What companies in Nouadhibou are hiring for [job type] in 2024?"
> "What skills are most in demand for [job type] in Mauritania?"
> "Suggest 5 job search websites and Facebook groups for finding work in Nouadhibou."

## Writing a strong CV

**AI prompt for CV writing:**
> "I am a [age]-year-old from Nouadhibou applying for a [job type] position. My experience: [list 2-3 experiences]. My skills: [list skills]. My education: [list education]. Write a professional CV in [Arabic/French] that highlights my strengths for this job."

**CV rules:**
- Keep it to 1 page
- Put your strongest points at the top
- Use numbers when possible ("served 50 customers per day")
- Match your CV to each job you apply for

## Writing cover letters

**AI prompt:**
> "I am applying for [job title] at [company]. The job description says: [paste description]. Write a cover letter in [Arabic/French] that shows why I am a good fit. Mention my experience in [relevant area]. Keep it to 3 paragraphs."

## Interview preparation

**AI prompt:**
> "I have an interview for [job title] at [company]. The job involves [describe duties]. Give me 10 likely interview questions and suggest strong answers based on my background: [describe your background]."

**Practice with AI:**
Tell AI to act as the interviewer. Answer its questions, then ask for feedback.

## Following up

After an interview, send a thank-you message:
> "Thank you for the opportunity to interview for [position]. I am very interested in joining your team. Please let me know if you need any additional information. I look forward to hearing from you."

## Today's exercise

**Pick a job you want (real or imagined). Use AI to:**
1. Find 3 places to look for that job
2. Write a CV tailored for that job
3. Write a cover letter
4. Prepare answers to 5 likely interview questions

Save these documents. You now have a job application toolkit.
