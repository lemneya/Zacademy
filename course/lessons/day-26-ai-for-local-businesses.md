## Every business can use AI

Today we combine everything you have learned into a practical business improvement plan.

## The AI business audit

Pick a business (yours or a friend's). Answer these questions:

1. **Customer communication**
   - How do customers contact the business? (WhatsApp, Facebook, phone, in-person)
   - How fast are replies?
   - Could AI help write faster, better replies?

2. **Marketing**
   - How does the business find new customers?
   - Does it post on Facebook regularly?
   - Could AI help create more posts, better posts, faster?

3. **Products and pricing**
   - How are products described?
   - Are prices competitive?
   - Could AI help write descriptions or analyze competitors?

4. **Operations**
   - What tasks take the most time?
   - What tasks are repeated every day?
   - Could AI or automation save time?

5. **Growth**
   - What is the business's biggest goal this year?
   - What is stopping growth?
   - Could AI remove that barrier?
