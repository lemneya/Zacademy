# Day 19 — Perplexity

**Goal:** Learn to search with AI and get sources.

---

## What is Perplexity?

Perplexity is an AI search engine. Unlike ChatGPT, it:
- Searches the internet in real time
- Shows you the sources of its information
- Gives answers with links you can verify

## How to access Perplexity

1. Go to perplexity.ai
2. No account needed for basic use
3. Start searching

## Why Perplexity matters

Remember Day 5: AI makes mistakes. Perplexity helps you verify because it shows where the information comes from.

## How to use Perplexity

Ask questions like you would on Google, but in full sentences:

> "What are the current fishing regulations in Mauritania?"
> "How do I register a small business in Nouadhibou?"
> "What is the exchange rate between ouguiya and dollar today?"
> "Latest news about AI in Africa"

Perplexity will:
1. Search the web
2. Summarize what it found
3. Show you the links to the original sources

## Pro tips

**Focus your search:**
> "Site:gov.mr registration small business Mauritania"

**Ask for recent information:**
> "What happened in Nouadhibou in the last 30 days?"

**Compare sources:**
Click the links Perplexity provides. Do different sources agree?

## Today's exercise

**Use Perplexity to research 3 real questions about your life, business, or city. Verify the answers by clicking at least one source link for each.**

Write down:
1. The 3 questions
2. The answers Perplexity gave
3. Whether the source links were trustworthy
4. One thing you learned that you did not know before
