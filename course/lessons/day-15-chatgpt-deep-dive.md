## You are not a beginner anymore

By now, you have used ChatGPT for a week. Today we go deeper.

## Features you should know

**Custom Instructions:**
Tell ChatGPT who you are and how you want answers. Go to Settings → Custom Instructions.

Example:
> "I am a small business owner in Nouadhibou, Mauritania. I prefer answers in Arabic with French terms explained. Keep answers practical and short."

**GPTs (custom versions):**
ChatGPT has special versions for specific tasks: writing, coding, image creation, data analysis. Try them.

**Voice mode:**
On the mobile app, you can talk to ChatGPT instead of typing. Great for when you are driving or working with your hands.

**Memory:**
ChatGPT remembers things you tell it across conversations. Tell it important facts about your business once, and it will use them later.

## Advanced prompting

**Chain of thought:**
Ask AI to think step by step.
> "I want to start a small restaurant in Nouadhibou. Think step by step: what do I need to do in weeks 1, 2, 3, and 4?"

**Role-play:**
> "Act as an experienced business consultant who has helped restaurants in West Africa. Review my plan and tell me the 3 biggest risks."

**Compare options:**
> "I am choosing between selling fish online or opening a small shop. Compare the two options on: startup cost, daily time required, risk level, and profit potential. Give me a table."
