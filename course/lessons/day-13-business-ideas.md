# Day 13 — Business Idea Generation

**Goal:** Use AI to find new income opportunities suited to Nouadhibou.

---

## AI as a business partner

You do not need to come up with business ideas alone. AI can:
- Suggest ideas based on your skills and location
- Analyze what is working in similar cities
- Help you evaluate risks and costs
- Write simple business plans

## Prompts for business ideas

**Based on your skills:**
> "I am good at [skill] and I live in Nouadhibou. Suggest 5 business ideas I could start with less than 50,000 ouguiya. For each idea, tell me: what I need to start, who my first customers would be, and how much I could earn in the first month."

**Based on local needs:**
> "What services are missing or under-supplied in Nouadhibou? Suggest 5 business ideas that solve real problems for local people. Keep startup costs low."

**Based on AI services:**
> "What AI-related services could I sell to small businesses in Nouadhibou? I know how to use ChatGPT. Suggest 5 services, what to charge, and how to find my first 3 customers."

## Evaluating ideas

Not every idea is good. Use this filter:

1. **Can I start this week?** If not, it is too big.
2. **Do people in Nouadhibou already pay for this?** If not, you must educate the market — harder.
3. **Can I do it with what I already have?** Low startup cost = lower risk.
4. **Can AI help me do it faster or better?** If yes, you have an advantage.

## Today's exercise

**Use AI to generate 10 business ideas for yourself or someone you know. Filter them using the 4 questions above. Pick the top 3.**

Write down:
1. Your top 3 ideas
2. Which one you (or they) could start this month
3. The first 3 steps to start
