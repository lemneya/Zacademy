## The final project

Create a portfolio that shows you can use AI to solve real problems. Your portfolio must include:

### Required pieces (submit all 5)

1. **Your personal AI assistant prompt** (from Day 7, improved)
   Show how you customized AI for your life.

2. **One business marketing piece** (from Week 2, improved)
   A Facebook post, WhatsApp message, product description, or customer reply.

3. **One tool comparison** (from Week 3)
   Show that you tested multiple AI tools and know when to use each.

4. **One income project** (from Week 4)
   Either:
   - A service you are ready to sell, or
   - An AI improvement plan for a real business, or
   - A CV and cover letter for a real job application

5. **Your personal AI toolkit** (from Day 27)
   Show that you have organized your learning for continued growth.

### Bonus pieces (optional, but impressive)

- A video of you explaining one AI concept to a friend or family member
- A screenshot of a real customer message you improved with AI
- A document you translated or rewrote with AI
- A business idea you developed with AI and are considering

## How to submit

Send your portfolio to your Zidni Academy instructor via WhatsApp or email. Include:
- Your full name
- Your neighborhood in Nouadhibou
- The 5 required pieces
- Any bonus pieces
