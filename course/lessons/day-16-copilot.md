# Day 16 — Microsoft Copilot

**Goal:** Learn to use Microsoft's free AI assistant.

---

## What is Copilot?

Copilot is Microsoft's AI assistant. It is free and works on:
- The web (copilot.microsoft.com)
- Windows computers (built-in)
- Microsoft Edge browser
- Mobile apps

## Why use Copilot?

- It is free and does not require a phone number in all regions
- It can search the internet for current information
- It can create images from text descriptions
- It works well in Arabic, French, and English

## How to start

1. Go to copilot.microsoft.com
2. Sign in with a Microsoft account (free to create)
3. Start chatting

## What Copilot can do

**Search the web:**
> "What is the weather forecast for Nouadhibou this week?"
> "Find me news about fishing prices in Mauritania."

**Create images:**
> "Create an image of a busy fish market in Nouadhibou at sunrise."

**Analyze documents:**
Upload a PDF or Word document and ask questions about it.

**Help with Excel:**
> "I have a list of sales numbers. Help me calculate total profit."

## Copilot vs ChatGPT

| Feature | ChatGPT | Copilot |
|---|---|---|
| Free version | Yes | Yes |
| Internet access | No (free) | Yes |
| Image creation | Limited | Yes (DALL-E) |
| Mobile app | Yes | Yes |
| Arabic quality | Good | Good |
