# Day 3 — Prompting Basics

**Goal:** Learn to ask AI questions that get useful answers.

---

## What is a prompt?

A **prompt** is simply the message you send to AI. The quality of your prompt determines the quality of the answer.

Bad prompt → Bad answer
Good prompt → Good answer

## The 3-part prompt formula

Every strong prompt has three parts:

1. **Context** — Who are you? Where are you? What is the situation?
2. **Task** — What exactly do you want AI to do?
3. **Format** — How do you want the answer? (Short? Long? List? Table? Arabic? French?)

## Examples

**Weak prompt:**
> "Write about fish."

**Strong prompt:**
> "I am a fish seller at the Port de Peche in Nouadhibou. I want to post on Facebook to sell fresh fish to families. Write a short post in Arabic, friendly and local, that makes people want to buy. Keep it under 100 words."

**Weak prompt:**
> "Help me with my CV."

**Strong prompt:**
> "I am a 24-year-old from Nouadhibou applying for a customer service job at a telecom company. I have 2 years of shop experience and I speak Hassaniya, Arabic, and French. Write a professional CV summary in French, 3-4 sentences, highlighting my language skills and customer experience."

## Today's exercise

**Take one weak prompt and rewrite it using the 3-part formula. Test both versions in ChatGPT. Compare the answers.**

Weak prompts to choose from:
- "Tell me about business"
- "Help me study"
- "Write a message"
- "Explain money"

Write down:
1. Your weak prompt
2. Your strong prompt
3. Which answer was better and why
