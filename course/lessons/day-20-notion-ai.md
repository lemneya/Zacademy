# Day 20 — Notion AI

**Goal:** Organize your work and life with an AI-powered notes app.

---

## What is Notion?

Notion is a free app for notes, documents, to-do lists, and databases. Think of it as a digital notebook that can do everything.

Notion AI is the built-in AI assistant that helps you:
- Write and edit notes
- Summarize long documents
- Create to-do lists from meeting notes
- Translate and rewrite text
- Brainstorm ideas

## How to start

1. Go to notion.so
2. Create a free account
3. Download the mobile app
4. Create your first page

## What you can build in Notion

**Personal dashboard:**
- Daily to-do list
- Goals for the month
- Notes from this course
- Business ideas

**Business workspace:**
- Customer list
- Product inventory
- Content calendar for Facebook posts
- Financial tracker

**Project planner:**
- Step-by-step plans
- Deadlines and reminders
- Resources and links

## Notion AI prompts

> "Summarize this page into 3 bullet points."
> "Create a to-do list from these notes."
> "Rewrite this in more professional French."
> "Suggest 5 blog post ideas about AI in Mauritania."
