# Day 24 — AI for Students

**Goal:** Learn how students can use AI to study better, write better, and learn faster.

---

## AI is the best study partner

Students in Nouadhibou face unique challenges:
- Limited access to tutors
- Textbooks in French or English, not always Arabic
- Need to learn skills not taught in school
- Pressure to find jobs in a competitive market

AI can help with all of this.

## How students can use AI

**1. Understand difficult subjects**
> "Explain photosynthesis to me like I am 15 years old, in Arabic. Use examples from Mauritanian plants if possible."

**2. Practice languages**
> "I am learning French. Give me a 5-question quiz about daily conversations. Correct my answers and explain my mistakes."

**3. Write essays and reports**
> "I need to write a 500-word essay about the importance of fishing to Nouadhibou's economy. Give me an outline, then help me write each section."

**4. Prepare for exams**
> "Create 10 practice questions about [subject] at [level: middle school / high school / university]. Give me the answers after I try them."

**5. Learn new skills**
> "I want to learn basic graphic design using free tools. Give me a 7-day learning plan with specific tutorials and practice exercises."

**6. Find scholarships and opportunities**
> "What scholarships are available for Mauritanian students to study [subject] abroad? Give me a list with deadlines and requirements."

## Warning for students

**Do NOT:**
- Copy AI answers directly into homework (teachers can detect this)
- Use AI to cheat on exams
- Trust AI for factual answers without checking

**DO:**
- Use AI to understand, then write in your own words
- Use AI to practice and get feedback
- Use AI to find resources and opportunities
- Tell your teacher you are using AI to learn (honesty builds trust)

## Today's exercise

**If you are a student: pick one subject you are studying. Use AI to create a study plan for the next week.**

**If you are not a student: pick a topic you want to learn. Use AI to create a 7-day learning plan.**

Write down:

2. The 7-day plan AI created
