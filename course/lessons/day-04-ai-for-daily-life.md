# Day 4 — AI for Daily Life

**Goal:** Find 3 ways AI can save you time or stress this week.

---

## AI is not just for business

Most people think AI is for programmers or big companies. This is wrong. AI is for:
- Writing a difficult message to a family member
- Explaining homework to your child
- Planning a meal with what you have in the kitchen
- Understanding a document in French or English
- Making a shopping list
- Writing a complaint to a company
- Preparing questions for a doctor visit

## 10 daily-life prompts to try

1. **"Rewrite this message to be more polite but clear"**
   Paste a message you were afraid to send.

2. **"Explain this text in simple Arabic"**
   Paste a French or English document you did not understand.

3. **"I have [ingredients]. Suggest 3 meals I can cook."**

4. **"Help me plan my day. I need to do [tasks] in [hours]."**

5. **"Write a bedtime story in Hassaniya for a 5-year-old about a fish from Nouadhibou."**

6. **"Summarize this long message into 3 bullet points"**

7. **"I am nervous about [meeting/interview/conversation]. Help me prepare."**

8. **"Translate this between Arabic and French, keeping the tone friendly."**

9. **"My child asks: why is the sky blue? Explain simply."**

10. **"I want to learn about [topic]. Give me a 5-minute explanation for beginners."**

## Today's exercise

**Pick 3 prompts from the list above. Use them with ChatGPT today. Save the most useful answer.**

Write down:
1. Which 3 prompts you used
2. Which one saved you the most time or stress
3. One new idea you got from the conversation
