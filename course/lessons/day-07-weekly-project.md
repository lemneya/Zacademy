# Day 7 — Weekly Project: Personal AI Assistant Prompt

**Goal:** Create one powerful prompt you can use every day.

---

## What is a personal AI assistant prompt?

It is a detailed prompt you save and reuse. It tells AI who you are, what you do, and how you want help. With this prompt, every conversation starts stronger.

## Template

Copy this template and fill in your details:

---

**My Personal AI Assistant Prompt**

> I am [your name], a [your job/role] from [your neighborhood] in Nouadhibou, Mauritania. I speak [languages]. My daily work involves [describe your work in 2-3 sentences].
>
> I want you to act as my AI assistant. When I ask for help:
> - Give practical advice I can use today
> - Use examples from Mauritanian daily life when possible
> - If you use French or English terms, explain them in Arabic
> - Keep answers short unless I ask for detail
> - If you are not sure about something, say so honestly
>
> My goals this month are: [list 1-2 goals].

---

## Example (for a shop owner)

> I am Aicha, a clothing shop owner in the Cinquième neighborhood of Nouadhibou. I sell women's and children's clothes. I buy stock from Nouakchott and sell through my shop and WhatsApp. I speak Hassaniya and Arabic.
>
> I want you to act as my AI assistant. When I ask for help:
> - Give practical advice I can use today
> - Use examples from Mauritanian daily life when possible
> - If you use French or English terms, explain them in Arabic
> - Keep answers short unless I ask for detail
> - If you are not sure about something, say so honestly
>
> My goals this month are: sell more through Facebook, and write better messages to my suppliers.

## Today's exercise

**Write your personal AI assistant prompt using the template above. Test it in ChatGPT. Ask it 3 questions about your work or life. Save the prompt somewhere you can find it (notes app, WhatsApp message to yourself, or a notebook).**
