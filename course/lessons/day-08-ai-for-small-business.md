# Day 8 — AI for Small Business

**Goal:** Find 3 ways AI can help your business this week.

---

## AI is a business multiplier

You do not need to be a tech company to use AI. A small shop, a taxi driver, a tailor, a restaurant — all can use AI to:
- Save time
- Reduce costs
- Find more customers
- Make better decisions

## 10 ways AI helps small business

1. **Write Facebook and WhatsApp posts** to sell products
2. **Answer customer questions** faster and more professionally
3. **Create product descriptions** that make people want to buy
4. **Plan promotions and sales** for holidays and events
5. **Write messages to suppliers** to negotiate better prices
6. **Track simple finances** and calculate profit
7. **Generate business ideas** for new income streams
8. **Write job descriptions** when you need to hire
9. **Create simple flyers** and announcements
10. **Analyze what is working** and what is not in your business

## A Nouadhibou example

**Before AI:**
A restaurant owner spends 30 minutes writing a Facebook post about today's lunch menu. The post is boring. Three people see it.

**With AI:**
She asks ChatGPT: "Write an appetizing Facebook post for my restaurant in Nouadhibou. Today's special is grilled fish with rice and salad. Make it sound local and friendly."

AI writes the post in 10 seconds. She copies it, adds a photo, and posts. The post gets 50 views and 5 orders.

## Today's exercise

**Pick one area of your business (or a business you know). Ask AI for 3 specific ideas to improve it.**

Use this prompt:
> "I run a [type of business] in Nouadhibou. My biggest challenges are [list 2 challenges]. Give me 3 practical ways AI can help me this week, with specific examples."

Write down:
1. The 3 ideas AI gave you
2. Which one you will try first
3. What result you expect
