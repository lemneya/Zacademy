# Day 1 — What is AI?

**Welcome to Zidni Academy.**

Today we answer the simplest and most important question: What is AI, really?

---

## The simple answer

AI (Artificial Intelligence / الذكاء الاصطناعي) is a computer system that can learn from examples and make decisions without being told exactly what to do for every situation.

Think of it like this:
- A calculator does exactly what you tell it: 2 + 2 = 4
- AI learns patterns from millions of examples, then guesses the answer for new situations

## A Nouadhibou example

Imagine a young man who has worked at the fish market since he was 15. He can look at a fish and tell you:
- Is it fresh?
- What price should it sell for?
- Will customers buy it today or tomorrow?

He learned this not from a book, but from **seeing thousands of fish** over the years.

AI works the same way. It "sees" millions of examples, learns the pattern, then makes predictions about new things it has never seen before.

## What AI can do today

- Write emails, messages, and essays
- Answer questions in Arabic, French, and English
- Create images from descriptions
- Translate between languages
- Help with math and business calculations
- Suggest business ideas and marketing text

## What AI cannot do

- It cannot think like a human
- It cannot feel emotions
- It cannot know what is happening right now in Nouadhibou (unless you tell it)
- It sometimes makes mistakes — you must check its work
