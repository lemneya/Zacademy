# Day 14 — Weekly Project: Local Business Marketing Post

**Goal:** Create a complete marketing package for a real or imagined Nouadhibou business.

---

## What you will build

A complete marketing package with:
1. One Facebook post
2. One WhatsApp message for customers
3. One product description
4. One customer reply template

## Choose your business

Pick a real business in Nouadhibou (yours, a friend's, or one you imagine):
- Restaurant
- Clothing shop
- Fish seller
- Phone repair
- Tailor
- Grocery store
- Any other local business

## Step-by-step

**Step 1: Write the business brief**
Answer these questions:
- What is the business?
- Where is it located?
- Who are the customers?
- What is the main product or service?
- What makes it special?
- What is the price range?

**Step 2: Use AI to create each piece**

For each piece, use the prompts you learned this week:
- Facebook post (Day 10)
- WhatsApp message (Day 9)
- Product description (Day 11)
- Customer reply template (Day 12)

**Step 3: Review and improve**
Read everything together. Does it sound like the same business? Is the tone consistent? Edit where needed.

## Today's exercise

**Create the full marketing package. Submit all 4 pieces. If possible, show them to the actual business owner and ask for feedback.**

**Submit:**
1. Business brief
2. Facebook post
3. WhatsApp message
4. Product description
5. Customer reply template
