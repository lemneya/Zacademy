# Day 6 — AI for Arabic, French, and English

**Goal:** Use AI as your language assistant and translator.

---

## AI is a language bridge

For many people in Nouadhibou, the biggest barrier to using technology is language. Most online tools are in English or French. AI changes this.

You can:
- Write in Arabic, get answers in Arabic
- Write in Hassaniya (with some limitations), get answers in Arabic or French
- Ask AI to translate between any languages
- Ask AI to explain grammar or vocabulary
- Ask AI to rewrite something in simpler or more formal language

## Prompts for language work

**Translation:**
> "Translate this business message from French to formal Arabic: [paste text]"

**Simplification:**
> "Rewrite this in simple Arabic that a 12-year-old can understand: [paste text]"

**Formalization:**
> "Rewrite this WhatsApp message to sound more professional for a business email: [paste text]"

**Learning:**
> "Explain the difference between 'since' and 'for' in English, with examples related to business."

**Hassaniya support:**
> "Translate this Arabic text into Hassaniya dialect: [paste text]"

Note: AI knows Hassaniya better than most global tools, but it is not perfect. Always check with a native speaker for important messages.

## Today's exercise

**Find one message, document, or post you needed to write in another language. Use AI to translate or improve it. Show it to someone who speaks that language and ask if it sounds natural.**

Write down:
1. What you translated or wrote
2. Whether the AI version was better than what you would have written alone
3. One language skill you want to improve using AI
