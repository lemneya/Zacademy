# Day 22 — AI Services You Can Sell

**Goal:** Identify 5 AI services you can start selling this month.

---

## The opportunity

You have spent 3 weeks learning AI. Many people in Nouadhibou have not. That gap is a business opportunity.

You can sell AI services to:
- Small businesses that need help with social media
- Professionals who need better CVs and profiles
- Students who need help with writing and research
- Shops that need product descriptions and customer replies
- Anyone who needs translation or content creation

## 10 AI services to sell

1. **Social media management**
   Write Facebook posts, create content calendars, reply to comments.
   Price: 5,000–15,000 ouguiya per month

2. **Business writing**
   Product descriptions, WhatsApp messages, email templates.
   Price: 500–2,000 ouguiya per piece

3. **CV and profile optimization**
   Write professional CVs and LinkedIn profiles.
   Price: 2,000–5,000 ouguiya per CV

4. **Translation services**
   Translate between Arabic, French, and English.
   Price: 500–1,000 ouguiya per page

5. **AI training for businesses**
   Teach small business owners how to use ChatGPT for customer service.
   Price: 5,000–10,000 ouguiya per session

6. **Content creation**
   Write blog posts, newsletters, or website content.
   Price: 1,000–3,000 ouguiya per article

7. **Photo and image descriptions**
   Write captions and descriptions for product photos.
   Price: 200–500 ouguiya per product

8. **Customer service templates**
   Create reply templates for common customer questions.
   Price: 3,000–7,000 ouguiya for a full set

9. **Business idea consulting**
   Help people brainstorm and plan business ideas using AI.
   Price: 2,000–5,000 ouguiya per consultation

10. **AI-assisted research**
    Research competitors, prices, or market trends.
    Price: 2,000–5,000 ouguiya per report

## How to find your first customers

1. **Start with people you know.** Friends, family, neighbors.
2. **Post on your Facebook and WhatsApp status.** "I now offer [service]. First customer gets a discount."
3. **Visit local businesses.** Show them what AI can do. Offer a free sample.
4. **Ask for referrals.** Every happy customer can bring you two more.

## Today's exercise

**Pick 3 services from the list above that match your skills. Write a simple offer post for each. Post one on Facebook or WhatsApp.**

Write down:
1. The 3 services you chose
2. Your offer post
3. Who your first customer might be
