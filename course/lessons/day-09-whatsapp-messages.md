# Day 9 — WhatsApp Customer Messages

**Goal:** Write faster, better customer messages using AI.

---

## WhatsApp is your shop window

In Nouadhibou, WhatsApp is not just for chatting with family. It is how businesses:
- Take orders
- Answer questions
- Confirm deliveries
- Handle complaints
- Build trust with customers

Every message is a chance to win or lose a customer.

## Common WhatsApp situations

**Situation 1: Price question**
Customer: "How much is the fish?"

Weak reply: "500"
Strong reply: "Hello! Fresh fish from this morning at the Port de Peche — 500 ouguiya per kilo. We deliver to your door in Nouadhibou. How much would you like?"

**Situation 2: Delayed delivery**
You promised delivery at 2 PM. It is now 4 PM.

Weak reply: "Sorry, late."
Strong reply: "I sincerely apologize for the delay. Your order is on its way and will arrive within 30 minutes. As an apology, I am adding a small discount to your next order. Thank you for your patience."

**Situation 3: Complaint**
Customer: "The product is not what I expected."

Weak reply: "It is what we have."
Strong reply: "I am sorry the product did not meet your expectations. Your satisfaction matters to me. Can you tell me what was different from what you expected? I will find a solution for you today."

## How to use AI for WhatsApp

Prompt template:
> "A customer sent me this message: '[paste customer message]'. I run a [business type] in Nouadhibou. Write a professional, warm, and helpful reply in [Arabic/French]. Keep it under 80 words."

## Today's exercise

**Find 2 real or imagined customer messages. Use AI to write strong replies for both. Compare them to what you would have written without AI.**

Write down:
1. The original customer messages
