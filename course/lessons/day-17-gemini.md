# Day 17 — Google Gemini

**Goal:** Learn Google's AI assistant and compare it to others.

---

## What is Gemini?

Gemini is Google's AI assistant. It replaces the old Google Bard. It is free and works on:
- The web (gemini.google.com)
- Android phones (built-in)
- Google apps (Gmail, Docs, etc.)

## Why use Gemini?

- Deep integration with Google services
- Can search Google for current information
- Strong in Arabic and French
- Works with Google Docs, Sheets, and Gmail

## How to start

1. Go to gemini.google.com
2. Sign in with your Google account
3. Start chatting

## Special features

**Gmail integration:**
> "Summarize the emails I received this week about orders."

**Google Docs integration:**
> "Help me write a formal letter in French for my business."

**YouTube summaries:**
Paste a YouTube link and ask Gemini to summarize the video.

**Image understanding:**
Upload a photo and ask questions about it.
> "What is written on this sign?" or "What kind of fish is this?"

## Today's exercise

**Use Gemini for 3 tasks. Compare to ChatGPT and Copilot.**

Suggested tasks:
1. Ask the same question to all three AIs. Which answer is best?
2. Upload an image and ask about it
3. Ask for help with a Google Doc or Sheet

Write down:
1. The 3 tasks
2. Which AI won each task
3. Your personal ranking: ChatGPT, Copilot, or Gemini?
