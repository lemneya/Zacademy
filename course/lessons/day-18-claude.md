# Day 18 — Claude

**Goal:** Learn Anthropic's Claude and when to use it.

---

## What is Claude?

Claude is an AI assistant made by a company called Anthropic. It is known for:
- Very long, detailed answers
- Strong writing quality
- Excellent at analyzing long documents
- Careful and honest about uncertainty

## How to access Claude

1. Go to claude.ai
2. Create an account with your email
3. Start chatting

Note: Claude may not be available in all countries. If you cannot access it, use a VPN or skip to the next tool.

## When to use Claude

**Use Claude when:**
- You need to analyze a long document (up to hundreds of pages)
- You want the highest-quality writing
- You are working on a complex project that needs detailed planning
- You want an AI that admits when it is unsure

**Use ChatGPT when:**
- You want fast, practical answers
- You need voice mode or mobile app features
- You want the largest ecosystem of tools

## Claude's special feature: Projects

Claude lets you create "Projects" where you upload documents, set instructions, and have ongoing conversations about a specific topic. Great for:
- Planning a business
- Writing a book or course
- Analyzing legal or financial documents

## Today's exercise

**If you can access Claude, use it for one complex task. Compare the depth of its answer to ChatGPT.**

Suggested task:
> "I want to write a 3-month business plan for [your business idea]. Create a detailed week-by-week plan with specific actions, costs, and expected results."

Write down:
1. The task you gave Claude
2. How its answer compared to ChatGPT
3. When you would choose Claude over other AIs
