# Day 12 — Customer Service Replies

**Goal:** Handle difficult customer situations with AI-assisted professionalism.

---

## Every complaint is a chance

A customer who complains is a customer who still cares. If they did not care, they would simply leave and never return.

Your job is to turn complaints into loyalty.

## The HEARD method

Use this framework for every complaint:

- **H**ear — acknowledge their frustration
- **E**mpathize — show you understand
- **A**pologize — take responsibility
- **R**esolve — offer a specific solution
- **D**iagnose — prevent it from happening again

## AI prompt for complaint replies

> "A customer sent me this complaint: '[paste complaint]'. I run a [business type] in Nouadhibou. Write a reply using the HEARD method: Hear, Empathize, Apologize, Resolve, Diagnose. Keep it warm, professional, and under 120 words. Write in [Arabic/French]."

## Example: Late delivery complaint

Customer: "I ordered yesterday and still have not received anything. This is unprofessional."

AI-assisted reply:
> "Thank you for telling me directly. I understand how frustrating it is to wait for something you need — your time is valuable, and I did not meet the standard I promised. I sincerely apologize. Your order is my priority today: it will be delivered by 6 PM, and I am waiving the delivery fee. I am also reviewing my delivery process so this does not happen again. Thank you for your patience."

## Difficult situations

**The customer is wrong:**
Still apologize for their frustration. Then gently explain the facts.

**The customer is rude:**
Stay professional. Do not match their tone. Other people are watching.

**The customer wants a refund you cannot afford:**
Offer alternatives: store credit, replacement, discount on next order.
