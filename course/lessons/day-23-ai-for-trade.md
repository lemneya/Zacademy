# Day 23 — AI for Trade and Suppliers

**Goal:** Use AI to negotiate better, communicate faster, and find new suppliers.

---

## Trade is Nouadhibou's life

Whether you sell fish, import goods from Nouakchott, or trade with Senegal, communication with suppliers is critical.

AI can help you:
- Write professional negotiation messages
- Translate between languages
- Compare supplier offers
- Track orders and follow up
- Research new suppliers and products

## Writing supplier messages

**Requesting a better price:**
> "I have been buying from you for 6 months. Your quality is good, but your price is 10% higher than another supplier. Can you match their price, or add free delivery? I want to continue our relationship."

**Following up on a delayed order:**
> "I am checking on order #[number] placed on [date]. My customers are waiting. Can you confirm the delivery date? If there is a delay, please let me know as soon as possible so I can plan."

**Introducing yourself to a new supplier:**
> "My name is [name]. I run a [business type] in Nouadhibou with [number] regular customers. I am looking for a reliable supplier of [product]. Can you share your catalog and pricing? I am interested in a long-term partnership."

## AI prompt for supplier communication

> "I need to write a [type of message] to a supplier. The situation is: [describe situation]. I want to be [polite/firm/urgent] but maintain a good relationship. Write the message in [Arabic/French]. Keep it professional and under 100 words."

## Comparing offers

When you have multiple supplier offers, use AI to compare:

> "I have two supplier offers:
> Supplier A: [details]
> Supplier B: [details]
> Compare them on: price, delivery time, minimum order, payment terms, and risk. Give me a recommendation."
