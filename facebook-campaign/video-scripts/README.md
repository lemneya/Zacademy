# Video Scripts — Zidni Academy Facebook Campaign

## How to use these scripts

- Each script is 60-90 seconds when read at natural pace
- Film in Arabic (Hassaniya or Modern Standard Arabic)
- Film in natural settings: your shop, the street, the port, your home
- Subtitles in Arabic are essential (many watch without sound)
- Post in evening (7-8 PM) for maximum engagement
