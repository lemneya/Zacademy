# 30-Day Facebook Campaign — Zidni Academy

**Month 1: Identity Launch**
**Goal:** Reintroduce the page, establish daily cadence, rebuild trust, seed audience for Month 2 challenge.

---

## Daily Content Formula

Every day, 3 posts:

1. **Morning (8-9 AM):** Emotional Nouadhibou + knowledge post — local pride tied to one AI insight
2. **Afternoon (2-3 PM):** Practical AI lesson — one tool, one prompt, one outcome, finishable in minutes
3. **Evening (7-8 PM):** Short video, proof, testimonial, or challenge post — face on camera, student work, or CTA

## Rules
- Never skip a day. Cadence is the campaign.
- Local references over global references.
- Plain Arabic + French where helpful.
- Avoid technical jargon.
- Teaching tone over selling tone.
