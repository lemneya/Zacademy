# Deliverable 6 — Payment Collection SOP
## COHORT-ZERO-0
### Program Price: 500 MRU — Manual Collection (No Automation)

---

## Overview

Since no automated payment system is required, all payment collection is done manually by the Founder. This SOP covers the complete workflow from request to confirmation.

**Key rule:** Payment is collected at the end of the 7-Day Challenge, when the student decides to enroll in the full 28-Day Certificate Program. The challenge itself is free.

---

## Payment Workflow

```
Student completes Day 7
         │
         ▼
Founder sends upsell message
  "مبروك! تريد تكمل الدورة الكاملة؟"
         │
         ▼
Student says "نعم" or "دورة"
         │
         ▼
Founder replies with payment instructions
         │
         ▼
Student sends 500 MRU
  (Orange Money, Bank, or Cash)
         │
         ▼
Founder confirms receipt
  (Screenshot of transaction)
         │
         ▼
Founder marks Paid=YES in tracking sheet
         │
         ▼
Student receives:
  1. Confirmation message
  2. Receipt (WhatsApp text)
  3. Access to 28-day course materials
```

---

```
الدورة الكاملة (28 يوماً) تشمل:
📚 28 درساً عملياً متقدماً
🎓 شهادة مهارات الذكاء الاصطناعي
👥 مجتمع خريجي زدني أكاديمي

💰 السعر: 500 أوقية فقط — مرة واحدة
📲 أرسل "دورة" للتسجيل
```

## Step 2: Payment Instruction Message

When student says "دورة" or "نعم":

```
خطوات التسجيل في الدورة الكاملة:

1️⃣ حول 500 أوقية عبر Orange Money إلى الرقم: [FOUNDER PHONE NUMBER]
   (أو نقداً إذا قابلتني شخصياً)

2️⃣ أرسل لقطة شاشة لعملية التحويل هنا

3️⃣ سأؤكد استلام المبلغ وأرسل لك مواد الدورة

ملاحظة: التسجيل اختياري. لا يوجد أي التزام.
لكن إذا سجلت، التزم بإكمال 28 يوماً 💪
```

---

## Step 3: Payment Verification

**Orange Money (recommended):**
1. Founder checks Orange Money account for incoming 500 MRU
2. If student sent screenshot, verify the reference number matches the incoming transaction
3. Take your own screenshot of the confirmed balance as proof

**Bank Transfer:**
1. Check bank account
2. Match name/amount with student details
3. If names don't match (someone else sent it), ask student to confirm the sender name

**Cash:**
1. Receive cash in person
2. Give handwritten receipt (or WhatsApp confirmation)
3. Immediately mark in tracking sheet

---

## Step 4: Confirmation + Receipt Message

After payment is confirmed:

```
✅ تم استلام 500 أوقية من [STUDENT NAME]

أنت الآن مسجل في دورة زدني أكاديمي — 28 يوماً.

📅 موعد بدء الدورة: [COURSE START DATE]

شكراً لثقتك. نحن فخورون بك. 🙌

الموارد الطبيعية قد تنتهي، لكن المعرفة لا تنتهي.
```

## Refund Policy Recommendation

**Recommendation for Cohort Zero:** No refunds.

**Rationale:**
- 500 MRU is intentionally low to minimise financial risk
- The challenge (7 days) is free — the student already experienced the teaching style
- The cost is equivalent to one cheap meal in Nouadhibou
- Managing refunds manually is overhead the Founder should not carry at this stage

**If a student insists on a refund:**
Handle case-by-case. Founder's discretion. If refunded, document the reason and mark Refunded=YES in the tracking sheet.
