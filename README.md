# Zidni Academy

Zidni Academy is an AI education and public movement project designed to make **Nouadhibou** a leading AI-learning city in **Mauritania** — and from there, to spread practical AI literacy across the country.

We are not just selling a course. We are building a public movement around the idea that **knowledge, not natural resources, is the durable wealth of a nation**.

> Natural resources may end, but knowledge grows when shared.
>
> الموارد الطبيعية قد تنتهي، لكن المعرفة لا تنتهي.

---

## First product

**28-day beginner AI Skills Certificate** — a practical, accessible course that teaches everyday people (students, shop owners, traders, job seekers, teachers, drivers, entrepreneurs) how to use AI in their work and daily life.

## Public mission

Spread practical AI literacy across **Nouadhibou** first, then across **Mauritania**. The goal is not to make people consumers of AI, but participants in the AI era — people who learn, produce, and compete.

## Growth mission

Grow the Zidni Academy Facebook page from **7,000 followers to 100,000 followers in 6 months**, using a daily content engine, a 7-day free AI challenge, and city-level campaigns inside Nouadhibou.

## Strategic purpose

Zidni Academy is the **education and trust layer** of the larger **Zidni ecosystem** — a super-app and business platform Mohiyidine Cheikh is building for Mauritania. Education comes first because trust comes first.
