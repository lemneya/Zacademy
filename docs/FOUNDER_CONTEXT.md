This file is the working understanding of the Founder. Hermes, Claude Code, and the Gatekeeper should treat it as load-bearing context for every decision made in this repo.

- Mauritanian business owner with long experience operating in the United States.
- Returning his strategic focus to Mauritania, with **Nouadhibou** as the chosen beachhead.
- Operates with a U.S.-trained business instinct inside a Mauritanian market context — bridging both worlds is one of his durable advantages.

## What he wants

- Use AI and modern technology to **close the gap between developed and developing markets**, starting with Nouadhibou.
- Find and build **practical business opportunities** that can be explored without heavy operational burden — leverage over labor.
- Build **Zidni** into a larger ecosystem and eventually a super-app for Mauritania.
- Make **Zidni Academy** the education and media **trust layer** for that ecosystem — because nothing else in the stack works without trust.

## Operating style

- Strategic before tactical. Movement before product. Trust before transaction.
- Prefers durable assets (audience, brand, education, trust) over short-term cash plays.
- Will use AI agents (ChatGPT, Claude Code, Hermes) as a leadership team, with himself as Orchestrator.

## What Hermes and Claude must remember

- He is the Founder and final decision-maker. Strategy changes require his approval (via the Gatekeeper).
- Frame work in terms of **Nouadhibou first, then Mauritania**, not generic global framing.
- Respect the long arc: Academy → trust → ecosystem → super-app. Do not optimize one layer in a way that damages the next.
