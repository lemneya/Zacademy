# Facebook Growth Plan — 7,000 → 100,000 in 6 months

The Facebook page is the public engine of Zidni Academy. It is where the movement is visible, where the course recruits, and where the Founder's public identity is built.

---

## Targets

- **Starting point:** 7,000 followers
- **Target:** 100,000 followers
- **Window:** 6 months

## Monthly arc

### Month 1 — Identity launch

- Reintroduce the page as **Zidni Academy**.
- Publish the manifesto: Nouadhibou must enter the AI era as a city that learns and produces, not just consumes.
- Establish daily posting cadence (see formula below).
- Goal: rebuild trust and seed the audience for Month 2.

### Month 2 — 7-day free AI challenge

- Free, public, daily-task AI challenge run on the page and WhatsApp.
- Each task is finishable in under 30 minutes by a beginner.
- Captures registrations and produces the first wave of public student proof.

### Month 3 — Paid 28-day course launch

- Open enrollment for the 28-day AI Skills Certificate.
- Use Month 2 challenge graduates as social proof.
- Price and registration flow handled through WhatsApp + Google Form.

### Month 4 — Student proof and testimonials

- Heavy publication of student-made work: marketing posts, product descriptions, AI-assisted side income.
- Each testimonial is a recruitment asset for the next cohort.

### Month 5 — City-level campaign

- Public campaign: **"1,000 people in Nouadhibou must learn AI."**
- Partnerships with schools, mosques, markets, and local media.
- Convert Nouadhibou's geography into a story the rest of Mauritania watches.

### Month 6 — National expansion

- Expand explicitly to Mauritania (Nouakchott, Rosso, Atar, etc.).
- Reposition the page as **a national AI education movement headquartered in Nouadhibou**.

---

## Daily content formula

Three posts per day, every day:

- **Morning:** emotional Nouadhibou + knowledge post — local pride tied to a one-idea AI insight.
- **Afternoon:** practical AI lesson — one tool, one prompt, one outcome, finishable in minutes.
- **Evening:** short video, proof, testimonial, or challenge post — face on camera, student work, or call-to-action.

Rules:

- Never skip a day. Cadence is the campaign.
- Local references over global references. Names of neighborhoods, markets, professions.
- Plain Arabic + plain French where helpful; avoid technical jargon.
