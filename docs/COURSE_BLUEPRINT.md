# Course Blueprint — 28-Day AI Skills Certificate

The first product of Zidni Academy. Twenty-eight days, four weeks, one weekly project, one final project. Designed for absolute beginners in Nouadhibou — taught in plain language, with examples that look like their daily life.

---

## Week 1 — AI Foundations

Goal: remove the fear, build the basic mental model.

- Day 1: What is AI?
- Day 2: ChatGPT basics
- Day 3: Prompting basics
- Day 4: AI for daily life
- Day 5: AI safety and verification
- Day 6: AI for Arabic / French / English
- Day 7: **Weekly project — personal AI assistant prompt**

## Week 2 — AI for Business and Work

Goal: turn AI into income or savings inside their existing life.

- Day 8: AI for small business
- Day 9: WhatsApp customer messages
- Day 10: Facebook posts
- Day 11: Product descriptions
- Day 12: Customer service replies
- Day 13: Business idea generation
- Day 14: **Weekly project — local business marketing post**

## Week 3 — AI Tools

Goal: stop being a one-tool user.

- Day 15: ChatGPT
- Day 16: Copilot
- Day 17: Gemini
- Day 18: Claude
- Day 19: Perplexity
- Day 20: Notion AI
- Day 21: Zapier / automation

## Week 4 — AI Income and Practical Projects

Goal: ship something real, earn or save something real.

- Day 22: AI services people can sell
- Day 23: AI for trade and suppliers
- Day 24: AI for students
- Day 25: AI for job seekers
- Day 26: AI for local businesses
- Day 27: Building a personal AI toolkit
- Day 28: **Final project**

---

## Production notes

- Each daily lesson is short, practical, and exercise-driven.
- Examples are anchored in Nouadhibou life (markets, transport, fishing trade, students, traders).
- Weekly projects are visible artifacts the student can show — these become the testimonial pipeline.
- The Final Project becomes part of the student's public Certificate evidence.
